About SOTA
Regulatory Requirements Management System
A tool for extracting standards, regulations, and guidance references.

Purpose

Our platform assists reviewers by automatically identifying relevant regulatory standards from uploaded device-requirement documents, improving consistency and reducing manual effort.

Intended use

This tool is intended to support internal review workflows. It is not a substitute for professional regulatory or compliance judgment.

AI disclaimer

All AI-generated outputs must be independently reviewed and validated by a qualified professional before being used in any regulatory, compliance, or submission context.
